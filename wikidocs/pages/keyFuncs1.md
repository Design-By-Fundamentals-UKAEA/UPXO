Key functionalities include the following:

1. **Grain structure (-- GS) generation (-- gsGen), characterisation (--gsChar) and analysis (--gsAn)**
> Supports 2D and 3D. Voronoi tessellation GS (-- VTGS) can be generated using a wide variety of seed point distribution specifications. Q-state Potts-model Metropolis algorithm based Monte-Carlo simulation based GS (-- MCGS) can be generated using a wide variety of in-built algorithms. Experimental EBSD (i.e. Electron Back Scatter Diffaction) GS can also be imported and modified.

2. **Grain structure manipulations**
> This supports grain structre feature (A) removal, (B) introduction, (C) replacer and (D) modifier.

3. **Representativeness**
> Supports (A) self-GS and cross-GS representativeness assessments. Any combination of sample and target GS are supported for synthetic and experimental GS. Statistical and connectivity based assessments are supported.
